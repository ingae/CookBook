MRS. RORER'S CHOCOLATE CAKE

    2 ounces of chocolate,
    4 eggs,
    1/2 a cup of milk,
    1 teaspoonful of vanilla,
    1/2 a cup of butter,
    1-1/2 cups of sugar,
    1 heaping teaspoonful of baking powder,
    1-3/4 cups of flour.
	
MERINGUE

    2 eggs (whites),
    Pinch of salt,
    4 level tablespoonfuls of sugar,
    1 teaspoonful of vanilla.
